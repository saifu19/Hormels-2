Dependants and Documentation

The following people are eligible for health care and life insurance coverage as a dependant:

  • Spouse/Domestic Partner
  • Children up to age 26
    • Natural Born children
    • Stepchildren
    • Legally adopted children
    • Children in the process of adoption who have been plave in your home
    • Domestic partner's children up to age 26
    • Legal ward
  • Disabled children who meet certain requirements as stated in the Summary Plan Descriptions (health care coverage only)

You will need to PROVIDE DOCUMENTATION to verify your dependants' eligibility if adding them to coverage.

Examples of documentation1 include

1 Additional documentation may be requested

2 The 1st page of the tax form showing filing status is needed. Please black out any financial information.

3 These forms can be requested from the Horizon Solution Center.

How do I provide documents to the Horizon solution center ?

After logging into Oracle, from your Home screen, click on Me, then Benefits, and then Document Records. You’ll attach either a scan or a picture of the document to upload. Please upload one document at a time.

If you're using the mobile app, click on Me, then Benefits, and then Document Records. The app allows you to take a picture of the document and upload. This is a secure method that does not save the image to your mobile device.

Important Domestic partner information

The portion of the health care contribution for a domestic partner and/or domestic partner’s children will be aftertax and imputed income will apply. Consult your tax advisor for specific information regarding imputed income an possible tax consequences.
