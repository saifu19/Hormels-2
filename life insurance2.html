Basic Employee Life and Basic Employee Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D)

Updates to Basic Employee Life and Basic Employee Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D)

The company provided life and AD&D coverages are being updated so that all eligible employees will have the following coverage amounts:

  • Basic Employee Life - One times your annual salary up to $50,000
  • Basic AD&D - One times your annual salary up to $50,000

Optional survivor benefit insurance options

In the event of your death, this insurance provides a monthly benefit to your surviving spouse/domestic partner and/or eligible children. The monthly benefit is a percentage of the first $5,000 of your monthly earnings.

Spouse Only

Your spouse will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month

Children only

Your children will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month

Spouse and Children

Your spouse will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month

PLUS

your children will receive 15% of your montly earnings up toa maximum benefit of $750 per month

Evidence of insurability (EOI) will be required if coverage is not elected when you are first eligible to elect it.
