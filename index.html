Employee Life Insurance

Basic

Provided by the company at no cost to you.

1 times salary max of $50000

Optional

Offered to you to purchase
for additional financial security.

Choose as a times salary in the following options: 1/2 - 6 times your salary

(this will increase as your benefits salary increases.)

  • Maximum benefit is the lesser of 6x annual salary or $50000
  • Your contributions are based on your age at the end of next year and will increase as you get older
  • Evidence of insurability is proof of good health. It is required for any election over $250,000 when you are first eligible to elect coverage, or any future increase

Spouse Life Insurance

Basic

Provided by the company at no cost to you.

Not applicable

Optional

Offered to you to purchase
for additional financial security.

Life insurance for your spouse/domestic partner. $10,000 to $50,000 in $5,000 increments.

Contributions are based on your spouse's age at the end of next year and will increase as they get older

  • EOI not required when first eligible. EOI will be required on spouse for any future increase in coverage

Optional survivor benefit insurance

In the event of your death, this insurance provides a monthly benefit to your surviving spouse/domestic partner and/or eligible children. The monthly benefit is a percentage of the first $5,000 of your monthly earnings.

Evidence of Insurability (EOI) will be required if coverage is not elected when you are first eligible to elect it.

Spouse Only

Your spouse will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month

Children only

Your children will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month

Spouse and Children

Your spouse will receive 30% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $1,500 per month PLUS your children will receive 15% of your monthly earnings up to a maximum benefit of $750 per month

Adding or increasing coverage in the future will be subject to evidence of insurability. Aflac will mail you a health questionnaire to complete. Until Aflac determines approval or denial, the election will remain a pending status.
