Voluntary Benefits

Unum Critical Illness

When a serious illness strikes, Unum Critical Illness coverage can provide financial support to help you through a difficult time. It will pay a lump-sum cash benefit to offset the impact of a covered catastrophic illness. Some covered conditions include coronary artery disease, heart attack, stroke, some cancers, end stage renal failure and major organ failure requiring transplant. For more details, call (800) 653-5597.

Unum Accident & Injury

Unum Accident & Injury coverage provides payments for the diagnosis and treatment of covered off the job accidental injuries. If elected, employees receive the payment, regardless of other insurance coverage, to help offset a variety of out-of-pocket expenses ranging from medical plan deductibles and co-pays to ambulances, scans, X-rays, surgical procedures, physical therapy, and diagnosis of burns, dislocations, and fractures. You can also earn a wellness incentive by completing important tests and screenings. For more details, call (800) 653-5597.

Hyatt Legal Service

Hyatt Legal provides you access to affordable legal services by offering unlimited, full coverage if using an in-network attorney for covered matters. Some of the key covered services include immigration assistance, estate planning, family and personal services including divorce, enforcement or modifications of support orders, real estate services, traffic ticket defense and identity management services. This benefit provides multilingual interaction with attorneys through office visits, phone, email, mail or fax. For more details, visit www.info.legalplans. com (code: 9900372) or call (800) 821-6400.

Nationwide Pet Insurance

Nationwide Pet insurance offers medical and wellness plans for dogs, cats, birds and exotic animals that are kept as household pets, offering reimbursement in the event veterinary care is needed for an accident or illness. The coverage provides 90% reimbursement on qualified expenses for receiving care and treatment and offers preferred pricing with multi-pet discounts. Members also have free, 24/7 access to a veterinary professional through Vet Helpline. Pet insurance is intended to protect a pet owner from financial loss when a significant veterinary bill becomes inevitable. To enroll in this benefit, you must visit the Nationwide website. For more information and to enroll, visit www.petinsurance.com/hormelfoods, or call (877) 738-7874.

Unum, Hyatt Legal, and Nationwide coverages are administered by these companies respectively. Payroll deductions will be once per month on the 1st paycheck of each month. The cost shown in Oracle is the amount that would deduct monthly.
