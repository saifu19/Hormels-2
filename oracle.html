Access & Navigation

New to Oracle ?

Web based access

  1. Visit yoursource.hormel.com
  2. Click on Oracle Cloud
  3. Follow instructions to login below

Mobile access

  1. Search “Oracle HCM Cloud” in your App Store or Google Play Store, download it and open it.
  2. When you open the app, you might see a legal terms screen. Review and hit agree.
  3. On the next screen, enter this address: ekkh.fa.us2.oraclecloud.com This initial log in is the only time you will have to enter this address.
  4. You might get a pop up asking to allow notifications. It is recommended to choose Allow so you can be made aware of notifications in the system.
  5. This will take you to the main log in page. Follow instructions to log in below.

First time logging in ?

If you have a company email, click the company email button and sign in using your company email and login information. If you do not have a company email, your username is your nine digit person number / employee ID.

If you do not have a company email, your username is your nine digit person number / employee ID. Your password will be what you had created. If you don't remember your password, contact your local HR office or call the Horizon Solution Center to add your email contact so a registration link can be emailed to you to register and set a password.
